Your privacy, our mission.

At GetzyForex, privacy and data protection are at the heart of everything we do. This policy describes, in detail, our practices and your rights.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

  • Name, email address, and identifiers for registration
  • Profile data from integrated platforms
  • Payment and subscription details
  • Communication preferences

Usage Data

  • Platform interactions and viewed content
  • Device, browser, and session analytics
  • Learning pathway and trading activity

How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver and personalize our trading and learning solutions
  • To process payments securely and manage your subscriptions
  • To communicate platform updates and security alerts
  • To analyze and improve user experience and product features
  • To comply with all legal and regulatory obligations

Data Security

  • Military-grade encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and in transit
  • Strict access controls and continuous monitoring
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing

Information Sharing

We never sell your data. We only disclose your information when:

  • You provide explicit consent
  • It is required by law or regulation
  • Service providers assist in delivering our services (always under strict agreements)

Your Rights

  • Request access to your data at any time
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep your experience secure, fast, and personalized.

Essential Cookies
Necessary for platform security, login, and session management.
Analytics Cookies
Help us improve the platform by analyzing usage trends (fully anonymized).
Preference Cookies
Remember your settings for a seamless experience.
